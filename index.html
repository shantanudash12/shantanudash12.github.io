01 — About

I'm a Power BI developer working on enterprise-scale analytics for a large consumer goods business — building and maintaining the dashboards that track performance, pricing, and competitive positioning across markets and product segments. Along the way I picked up a habit for chasing down the kind of bugs that hide in plain sight: unit mismatches, filter context leaks, model-level quirks that look like report bugs but aren't.

Over the past several months I've been deliberately building toward Data Engineering — Databricks, PySpark, and cloud data platforms — because I want to be closer to where the data actually gets shaped, not just where it gets displayed.
